## üìù Key Principles for Content Pillars

### ‚úÖ DO:
- Use specific, memorable names that reflect the brand
- Include concrete, actionable content ideas
- Reference specific formats, settings, or storytelling approaches
- Connect authentically to brand values and target audience
- Use culturally relevant language (Spanish where appropriate)
- Make examples vivid and specific, not generic

### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Use generic phrases like "Fresh & Premium" or "Authenticity"
- Reuse the same content pillars for every campaign
- Use vague descriptions like "engaging content" or "visual appeal"
- Copy-paste templates without customization
- Ignore the brand's unique positioning and campaign goals
