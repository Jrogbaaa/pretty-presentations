## üîç Key Learnings Across All Examples

### Spanish Agency Terminology
- **oleada** = wave/campaign iteration
- **contrastar** = confirm/validate
- **PDM** = presentation deadline  
- **PTE** = pending/to be confirmed
- **porfi** = por favor (please, informal)

### Common Email Patterns
- WeTransfer links for reference materials
- Instagram/TikTok handles for creator suggestions
- References to previous campaigns or team members
- Questions to agency ("¬øPodemos...?", "¬øLas ten√©is?")
- Casual sign-offs ("Abrazo!", "Mil gracias!!!")
- Emojis in greetings ("üôÇ", "üî•")

- Exact figures: "111.800‚Ç¨"
- Split budgets: "39.000‚Ç¨ + 5.000‚Ç¨"
- Scenarios: "30.000‚Ç¨ or 50.000‚Ç¨"
- Not confirmed: "no nos han confirmado"
- Limited: "no hay mucho budget"

### Timeline Patterns
- Specific: "October 6 - November 2"
- Uncertain: "probably October", "dates TBD"
- Deadlines: "Wednesday 29 before 10am"
- Phases: "Dec 5-25, Jan 15-30, Feb 2+"
